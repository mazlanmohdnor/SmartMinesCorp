ABOUT US

Smart Mines Capital (M) Sdn. Bhd. (SMCSB) was incorporated on July 10, 2015 with a registered capital of MYR 1,000,000.00 with Kuantan based specialised international company with many mining subsidiary entities overseas with a business scope ranges includes exploration, ore processing, mining, smelting , trade of mineral products and the operation of relevant industry.

The mineral products are Manganese (Mn), Iron Ore (Fe), and also Graphite(C). Apart from that, we also do other minerals such as Galena and Tungsten. However, Manganese and Graphite is the prominent mining sector over the other minerals and its easy to operate will lead to ensure of our economic growth. We as one company aiming for a high return to all stakeholders and ensure for the success in our mining company in the entire Malaysia. We also accept talent in the skills of geologist from Australia and China that provide guidance in ensuring minerals are extracted in high quality and meets all the requirements.

 

SMC also emphasise on eco-friendly by promoting sustainable growth and environmental responsibility. We go beyond legislative compliance to promote pragmatic environmental solution and practices in all of our operations.
